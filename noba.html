Noba: The Smart Birth Control Tracker

App + Product Design

Noba is an app-connected smart birth control case that reminds users of exactly when to take their birth control pills every day. The app helps users track their period, their mood, and how on time they take their birth control pills. To encourage users to take their pills on time, Noba offers prizes or gifts with their monthly or tri-monthly delivery of birth control pills.

Developed and designed in DESIGN 166 in March 2022 as a project for the prompt "Interactive and Impactful Design." I was the sole designer, developer, and editor of this project.
