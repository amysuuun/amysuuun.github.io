dnAMY by sunhousemailbox

Fashion Collection: Spring/Summer 2022

This was my freshman year collection for the MESH fashion show in May 2022, under the label "sunhousemailbox," a temporary label I used that was inspired by my high school biology teacher Mr. Pallone! "sunhousemailbox" refers to the structure of a DNA nucleotide, which looks like a house with a mailbox with a sun.

This collection is called dnAMY, a clever combination of “DNA” and my own name, dedicated to my refound passion for the arts and the beautiful community we now call MESH.

From left to right: Logan Hosoda, Ellis Thach, Amy Sun, Xiaolin Huang, Ned Wojcik.

Photography by: Megan Clark, Taiying P, Hazel Zhu.
