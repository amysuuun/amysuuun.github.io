SafetyNet

App Design

SafetyNet is a dating app safety app developed to assist women in potentially dangerous situations. The app serves as an extension to existing dating apps, providing users with the functionality to track potential dates, send date information to friends and family, and report incidents of dangerous dates.

This idea was developed over the course of 2 months in INFO 360: Design Methods through conducting various interviews, surveys, and user testing with people of the target audience.
