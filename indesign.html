InDesign: Virtual Fashion Shows

Fashion Organization + Project Management

Throughout 2020, organizations all around had trouble putting together events that were up to par as their pre-Covid dazzlers. As the president of Lynbrook High School's fashion design club, I went to through the same troubles, having our annual fashion shows cancelled twice in a row. To make up for these, I directed and produced two virtual fashion shows with the assistance of my talented officer team in videography and set design.

In total I directed two virtual fashion shows - a fall 2020 show to make up for the skipped spring 2020 show, and the 2021 virtual show titled "Written in the Stars," a full 40-minute production featuring all 5 regular categories of our shows, as well as filler acts by student singers, musicians, and dancers. Outside of directing, I also participated in these fashion shows as a model, designer, and later emcee for the full 2021 production. Videography and Editing done by Suanna Zhong and Austin Tong.
